## Description
A budgeting and oversight ASP that an agent calls before and after every x402 payment. Ledger tracks cumulative spend per category, flags when two ASPs are being paid for functionally the same thing, benchmarks price against comparable listings on OKX.AI, and can flag over-spend before it happens. Every agent that transacts on OKX.AI is a potential recurring customer.

## Details
- Category: Finance
- Service type: A2MCP
- Price: 0.05 USDT per call
- Endpoint: /track

## What you send
Payment details including ASP name, category, amount in USDT, and agent wallet address.

## What you get back
Cumulative spend summary, duplicate service warnings, price benchmark against similar ASPs, and a budget status flag.
